About Metafusion

关于元融

Metafusion (Shenzhen) Enterprise Management Co., Ltd. is a management consulting firm undergoing a comprehensive AI transformation. We have developed our own AI GC product line, dedicated to fusing fundamental business elements with artificial intelligence to create new forms of enterprise intelligence.

Our name embodies the philosophy that when foundational elements come together through innovative fusion, they give rise to something greater — new intelligence, new wisdom, and new possibilities for the businesses we serve.

元融（深圳）企业管理有限公司是一家正在全面推进 AI 转型的管理咨询企业。 公司已自主开发 AI GC 产品线，致力于将基础商业元素与人工智能相融合， 创造全新的企业智能形态。

元融之名蕴含了这样一种理念：当基础元素通过创新融合汇聚在一起， 便能创造出更伟大的事物——为我们服务的企业带来新智能、新智慧和新可能。
